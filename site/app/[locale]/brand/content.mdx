## Trademark Policy

“Master CSS”, the Master CSS logo, and associated marks are trademarks owned by Aoyue Design. They represent our project and our community. Please follow these rules to use them appropriately.

(o) You may:

- Refer to “Master CSS” in educational content, blog posts, tutorials, and open-source tools.
- Use phrases like “powered by Master CSS”, “compatible with Master CSS”, or “built with Master CSS”.
- Display our logo in blog posts, videos, UI mockups, and plugin documentation.
- Use it to indicate integration with or support for Master CSS.
- Resize proportionally while keeping the original aspect ratio.

(x) You may not:

- Use “Master CSS” or our logo in the name of your product, domain, or company (e.g. `mastercss-tools.io`, `Master CSS UI`).
- Present your project as “official” or “endorsed” unless we’ve formally partnered.
- Sell goods (e.g. T-shirts, stickers) featuring our logo without written consent.
- Modify the logo’s color, shape, or orientation.
- Add shadows, gradients, or other visual effects.
- Use our logo as part of your own logo or product identity.
- Place the logo on merchandise without express permission.

Please use our name in the correct format and spelling.
